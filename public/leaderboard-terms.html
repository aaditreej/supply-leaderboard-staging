About the Programme

The Listener Leaderboard is a daily in-app incentive programme for eligible listeners on the Dostt App. It ranks listeners by their total talk time each day and awards coins to top performers and those who maintain qualifying streaks.

03

How Rankings Work

Qualifying — A listener must accumulate at least 10 minutes of total talk time in a calendar day (IST) to be considered "qualified" for that day's leaderboard. Listeners who have not yet reached 10 minutes are shown a progress indicator but do not appear in the ranked pool.

Personal Pool — Each listener sees a personalised pool of 20 competitors drawn from nearby qualifiers. Your display rank (1–20) reflects your position within your own pool, not a global rank. Your pool is refreshed periodically throughout the day as talk times change.

Talk Time Data — Rankings are derived from talk time data sourced from our analytics system. Data may reflect activity from up to 10 minutes prior to the time of viewing. The Company's records shall be final and binding, except in cases of manifest error.

Your displayed rank is always 1–20 within your personal pool. The podium (top 3) shown on your screen represents the top 3 of your pool, not global top 3.

05

Daily Reset & Streak Cycle

Daily Reset — The leaderboard resets at midnight IST each day. Your talk time resets to zero, and rankings are recalculated fresh from the start of each new day. Podium rewards, if earned, are credited at this point based on your final pool rank for the completed day.

Streak Cycle — Your streak counter tracks consecutive days on which you qualified (≥10 minutes). A streak bonus of ₹500 in coins is credited upon completing every 7th consecutive qualifying day. Missing a qualifying day resets your streak to zero. A new streak cycle begins on the following qualifying day.

Streak counts are visible on the Streak tab in the leaderboard. Your streak is not lost during the day — it holds at your previous day's count and updates at midnight once today's qualification status is settled.
