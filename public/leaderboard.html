These Terms apply to the Listener Leaderboard programme operated by Behtar Technology Private Limited ("Company"). They must be read alongside the Terms of Use, Privacy Policy, and Community Guidelines. By participating you confirm you have read, understood, and accepted these Terms.

1. About the Programme

The Listener Leaderboard is a daily in-app incentive programme that ranks eligible listeners by their total talk time each calendar day (12:00 AM – 11:59 PM IST) and awards coins to top performers and those who maintain qualifying streaks. The Programme is a promotional engagement initiative and does not constitute a financial product or investment scheme.

2. Eligibility

Participation is available exclusively to registered listeners/experts on the Dostt App who hold a verified account linked to an active mobile number, are at least 18 years of age, and are in good standing. Accounts that are suspended, restricted, or under review are not eligible. The Company may verify eligibility at any time and restrict or revoke participation at its sole discretion.

3. How Rankings Work

A minimum of 10 minutes of total talk time is required to be "qualified" for that day's leaderboard. Each listener sees a personalised pool of 20 competitors drawn from nearby qualifiers. Your display rank (1–20) reflects your position within your own pool, not a global rank. The podium (top 3) shown on your screen represents the top 3 of your pool. Rankings are updated approximately every 10 minutes. The Company's records shall be final and binding except in cases of manifest error.

4. Rewards

Daily Podium Rewards — credited automatically at midnight IST based on your final pool rank:

  • 🥇 Rank 1 — ₹1,500 in coins
  • 🥈 Rank 2 — ₹1,000 in coins
  • 🥉 Rank 3 — ₹500 in coins

Streak Bonus — ₹500 in coins credited for every 7 consecutive days on which you qualify (≥10 minutes). Missing a qualifying day resets your streak to zero.

All rewards are credited as coins to your Dostt wallet and are subject to the standard coin expiry policy in the Dostt App Terms of Use. The Company reserves the right to delay, withhold, or reverse rewards in cases of suspected fraud or abuse.

5. Daily Reset & Streak Cycle

Talk time and rankings reset at midnight IST each day. Podium rewards for the completed day are credited at this point. Your streak counter tracks consecutive qualifying days; a 7-day streak bonus is credited upon completing every 7th consecutive qualifying day. Streak counts are visible on the Streak tab and hold at your previous day's count throughout the day, updating at midnight once settlement is complete.

6. Prohibited Conduct

Violations may result in reversal of rewards, disqualification, account suspension or termination, and legal action.
